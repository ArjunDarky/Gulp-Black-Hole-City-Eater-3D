# Gulp: Black Hole City Eater 3D

A mobile-ready 3D black hole city eater game built with Capacitor.

## Download

Play on Google Play:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.irozar.gulp

## Play

Open `index.html` in a browser, press **Start Run**, then drag or swipe on the canvas to move the hole.

## Implemented Roadmap Pieces

- Finger/mouse drag movement in a bounded arena.
- Objects with varied sizes and mass.
- Absorb rule: only objects smaller than the hole can be swallowed.
- Growth curve, score, 2-minute timer, game-over screen, and restart.
- Camera follow with gradual zoom-out as the player grows.
- Absorb juice: shrink-and-pull animation, particle bursts, screen shake, and generated Web Audio pops.
- Basic single-player .io flavor with two bot holes competing for smaller objects.
